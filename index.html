      Help

      • Search: Type a query, then press Enter or tap the magnifier.
      • Menu (LSK): Opens Favorites, History, Settings and Help. Press number keys 1-4 to jump to an item.
      • Back (RSK): Returns to the previous screen, or closes StartPage from Home.
      • Favorites: Add a title and URL, then tap Save Favorite.
      • History: Shows recently visited links. Tap Clear History to erase it.
      • Settings: Change the default search engine and toggle Day/Dark theme.
